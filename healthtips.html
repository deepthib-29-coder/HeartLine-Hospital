Daily Health Tips

Simple Habits for a Healthy Life

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 2–3 liters of water every day.

🥗 Eat Healthy

Include fresh fruits and vegetables in your daily meals.

🏃 Exercise Daily

Spend at least 30 minutes exercising every day.

😴 Sleep Well

Get 7–8 hours of quality sleep every night.

🩺 Regular Checkups

Visit your doctor regularly for preventive healthcare.

🧼 Maintain Hygiene

Wash your hands frequently and maintain personal hygiene.
